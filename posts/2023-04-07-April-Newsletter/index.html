Next workshop – Friday April 21st, 1-3pm

Raster and Spatial Random Forest with Dr Jacinta Holloway-Brown

Dr Jacinta Holloway-Brown will be providing a workshop on using the Stochastic spatial random forest SS-RF method for filling missing data in satellite images due to clouds, and also going over using raster2data for working with spatial image files (such as rasters), and converting these to dataframes in R in order to perform statistical analyses.
